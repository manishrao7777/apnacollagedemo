10 Lines on Lord Hanuman in English

>

  • Lord Hanuman is a Hindu deity known for his strength, devotion, and loyalty.
  • He is often depicted as a monkey god with a human-like face and body.
  • Hanuman is considered an avatar of Lord Shiva and a loyal servant of Lord Rama.
  • He played a crucial role in the Hindu epic Ramayana, helping Lord Rama rescue his wife Sita from the demon king Ravana.
  • Hanuman is also revered for his intelligence, wisdom, and knowledge of various scriptures.
  • He is worshipped by many Hindus as a symbol of courage, devotion, and selflessness.
  • Hanuman is believed to have the power to protect his devotees from evil and grant them strength and prosperity.
  • His devotees often recite the Hanuman Chalisa, a hymn composed in his honour, to seek his blessings.
  • Hanuman Jayanti, the birthday of Lord Hanuman, is celebrated with great fervour by Hindus all over the world.
  • Lord Hanuman is an embodiment of strength, devotion, and humility, and his teachings continue to inspire millions of people to this day.
  • Hanuman is believed to have the ability to change his form and size, and he can also fly.
